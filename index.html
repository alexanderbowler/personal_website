About

I build digital products with a bias toward clarity: simple flows, reliable systems, and interfaces that explain themselves. My work centers on the intersection of UX research, front-end development, and product strategy, especially for teams that need to turn complex information into confident decisions.

I care about the craft of documentation and the ethics of transparency. When I design a feature, I look for the smallest number of steps that still feel human. When I build a tool, I aim for the kind of stability that lets people forget the tool exists.

  • Accessibility as a baseline, not a bonus
